Scoring Pixels

Scoring Pixels is a new music company committed to bringing original music to Video Games and Movies. They provide music services such as original music scores, Audio recording, Sound Design, and Mastering.

Client

Scoring Pixels

Project type

Web Design

Project year

October 2017

The objective was to create a website that portrays the company's capabilities and its approach to music. The customer needed a place for potential clients to hear musical examples and facilitate the first interaction between a potential client and their team. We accomplished this by creating a simple layout with an emphasis on accessibility. We introduced a contact form and embedded analytics to help the clients' outreach efforts and enhance the user experience. We used a monochromatic palette to tone down visual stimulus overload and heighten the auditory perception.
