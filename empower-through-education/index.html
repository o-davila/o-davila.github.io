Empower ThroughEducation

Empower Through Education (ETE) is a non-profit organization focused on providing education and housing to South Sudanese girls. Donations made throughout the year allow the organization to provide school materials, housing, and food for the students.

Client

Empower Through Education

Project type

Web Design

Project year

December 2019

The objective was to provide a fresh look to the website and provide solutions on web traffic and SEO. After assessing the website we determined there were multiple problems with links not functioning. The structure of the site wasn’t well planned which lead to poor navigation and user experience. We presented the client with an option that simplified the navigation by combining similar content as well as reducing levels inside the hierarchy of pages. We also provided technical solutions regarding the performance of the website which ultimately helped speed up the page loads and bouncing rate from the site.
