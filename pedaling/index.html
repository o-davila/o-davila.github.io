Pedaling

Pedaling is a cycling application designed for bike commuting. It is an exploratory exercise of what a new bike commuter needs for everyday cycling in terms of navigation and fitness.

Client

Pedaling

Project type

UX Design

Project year

April 2021

The objective is to try to build one cohesive experience for bike commuters inside the iOS ecosystem. We needed to have navigation, fitness metrics, and engagement all in one platform while leveraging the use of an iPhone as a cycling computer and an Apple watch as a secondary device for data gathering.
